EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Eco Collective is an app that features resources to streamline your local low-waste journey, rather than being frustrated with local recycling information that is out of date and difficult to find.

MOBILE PROTOTYPE

Our UX team researched, prototyped, tested, and validated an idea for a brand-new mobile application: Eco Collective. This app serves to make recycling and being eco-conscious more accessible.

GOAL

Our UX team researched, prototyped, tested, and validated an idea for a brand-new mobile application: Eco Collective. This app serves to make recycling and being eco-conscious more accessible.

    RESEARCH

    We created a qualitative survey that garnered over 70 participants. Conducted 5 user interviews after sending out the survey. Not being able to recycle all of their items is a common frustration and not knowing where to go deepens that frustration.

    COMPETITOR ANALYSIS
    SUMMARY

    Our user is a young woman in her early 20’s. She is wanting to engage in a low waste impact lifestyle. Her goals are to shorten her google searches, know where to take hard to recycle items in her area. Our team created an empathy map to construct this persona.

    EMPATHY MAP
    PERSONA
    SUMMARY

    After defining our persona, we sketched out a storyboard and user scenario to see how this person would engage with our app. We then created a user flow and started deciding what our app needed.

    STORYBOARD
    USER FLOW
    SUMMARY

    We started with sketched wireframes and then moved them digitally. After we created our low-fidelity prototype, we conducted user tests to see if the flow was intuitive. We then iterated to make a clickable mid fidelity app, integrating a style guide.

    WIREFRAMES
    MID FIDELITY
    SUMMARY

    The feedback our user testing provided allowed us to make adjustments to our prototype.

    CHANGES

    The top five changes we made to better serve our users were:

  • Correcting the fonts for IOS.
  • We put in options for returning and new users.
  • Rather than using the map as a home screen we created a page with our main app features.
  • We made a page specifically for our app functions, to better serve our users.
  • We also created a payment verification page so that our users are confident knowing that we are keeping their information safe and confidential.
